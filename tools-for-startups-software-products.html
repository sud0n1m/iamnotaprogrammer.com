Here’s a quick list of software products, devices and tools I use and recommend to people in startups. I’m going to take some time to expand on these later:

  • Computer: Macbook Pro 13 in w/ large external Monitor
  • Browser: Chrome
  • Email / Calendar / Word Processing / Spreadsheets – Google Apps for Domains
  • 1 on 1 Chat: Chat in Gmail or Adium
  • Group Chat: Campfire with Propane
  • Office Phone: Google Voice (in Gchat or forwarded to a phone)
  • Personal Phone: iPhone
  • Text Editing: Textmate
  • Presentations: Apple Keynote
  • Wireframes: Apple Keynote with Keynote UX, Balsamiq Mockups or Omnigraffle
  • Screenshots: Skitch or LittleSnapper
  • Issue and Bug Tracking – Pivotal Tracker or a Cork Board with Notecards
  • Support – Tender Support
  • Personal Issue Tracking: Things
  • Password Management: 1 password
